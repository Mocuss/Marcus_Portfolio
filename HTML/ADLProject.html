Transport Classifier

For this solo project, my role was to create create 2 models, CNN and transfer learning models
to classify images, then compare the two models.

I was provided 7.5k pre-labelled images, divided equally among 3 classes.
The 3 classes were:

  • Airplane
  • Truck
  • Automobile

Images from the dataset were heavily modified,
only providing 32 by 32 pixels.

Dropout, regularization and Data Augmentation were used to prevent overfitting

CNN Model

The following are results of my CNN model.

  • Train Accuracy:0.9232
  • Test Accuracy:0.9060
  • Train Loss: 0.2175
  • Test Loss:0.2494
  • F1 Score:0.91

Confusion Matrix of my CNN model

My train and test Accuracy and Loss Curve

Transfer Learning Model

I first tested out different models to see which is best for my dataset.

After selecting the best model, I fined tuned my model
by unfreezing everything but the first 15 layers.

The following are results of my Transfer Learning model.

  • Train Accuracy:0.9945
  • Test Accuracy:0.9707
  • Train Loss: 0.0187
  • Test Loss:0.0859
  • F1 Score:0.9700
